**`SQUIB`** _(noun)_:

1. `a short humorous or satiric writing or speech`

 `a short news item`

2. `a small firecracker`

 `a broken firecracker in which the powder burns with a fizz`

3. `a small electric or pyrotechnic device used to ignite a charge`

---

**`SQUIB`** _(verb)_:

1. `to speak, write, or publish squibs`^[intr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intransitive_verb)

2. `to fire a squib`^[intr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Intransitive_verb)

3. `to utter in an offhand manner`^[tr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transitive_verb)

 `to make squibs against : LAMPOON`^[tr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transitive_verb)

4. `to shoot off : FIRE`^[tr](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Transitive_verb)
